Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 March 2026

Lunara ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Lunara mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using Lunara, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register.
  • Profile information: Display name, age, gender, relationship style, orientation, bio, profile photos, and other details you choose to provide.
  • Location data: Your approximate location (city/region) to show you nearby members. We never share your precise location with other users.
  • Photos and media: Profile photos and verification selfies you upload.
  • Voice recordings: Optional voice introductions you record.
  • Messages: Messages you send to other members through the app.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Lunara service
  • Match you with compatible members
  • Enable communication between members
  • Verify your identity and maintain community safety
  • Send you notifications about connections and messages
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Privacy Modes

Lunara gives you control over your visibility through three privacy modes:

  • Discreet: Your profile photo is blurred by default. Only members you connect with can see your photo.
  • Standard: Your profile is visible to other members. Your photo unlocks after mutual connection.
  • Open: Your full profile including photo is visible to all verified members.

You can change your privacy mode at any time in your profile settings.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Other users: Only the profile information you choose to make visible based on your privacy mode settings.
  • Service providers: Third-party services that help us operate Lunara, including Supabase (database), and cloud storage providers. These parties are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Verification Photos

Verification selfies submitted for identity verification are only viewed by Lunara administrators for the purpose of verifying your identity. They are never displayed on your profile or shared with other users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will result in the deletion of your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@getlunara.co.uk.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

9. Children's Privacy

Lunara is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Transfers

Your data is stored on servers located in the European Union (eu-west-1). If you are located outside the EU, your data may be transferred to and processed in the EU.

11. Third-Party Services

Lunara uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Expo/React Native: Mobile app framework
  • Resend: Email delivery
  • OpenAI: AI-powered features (premium users only)

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of Lunara after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
