Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Safety

Lunara is an adult platform for the ethically non-monogamous community. Protecting minors and maintaining a safe environment is our highest priority.

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

Lunara has a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behaviour that sexualises, exploits, or endangers minors. We are committed to full compliance with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including the reporting of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) to the appropriate authorities.

Lunara strictly prohibits any content, communication, or behaviour that involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. Violations result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement.

Age Verification and Access Controls

Minimum Age Requirements

  • Users must be 18 years or older to create an account on Lunara
  • Identity verification is required for all users before full access is granted
  • Age is declared at signup and verified through our identity verification process
  • App Store age restrictions enforced — rated 17+/18+ on both iOS and Android

Technical Safeguards

  • Email confirmation required for all new accounts
  • Photo verification with liveness detection to confirm user identity
  • AI-powered safety monitoring of conversation patterns
  • Admin review of all verification submissions before approval

Reporting Mechanisms

Every user on Lunara has access to in-app reporting and blocking tools. Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

How to Report

  • Tap any profile or message and select "Report" to flag inappropriate content
  • Use the Safety screen in the app to submit detailed safety concerns
  • Email us directly at hello@getlunara.co.uk for urgent safety matters
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours

Reporting to Authorities

  • Any CSAM discovered is immediately reported to NCMEC and relevant UK authorities
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • Accounts involved in CSAM are permanently banned and reported

Prevention Practices

  • Mandatory identity verification before accessing the platform
  • AI Safety Agent monitors conversations for grooming patterns
  • Photo moderation to detect and remove inappropriate imagery
  • User blocking and reporting available on every profile and conversation
  • Regular security audits of platform infrastructure

Compliance

Lunara complies with all applicable child safety legislation including the UK Online Safety Act, the US PROTECT Act, and EU regulations on child sexual abuse material.

Designated Safety Contact

Our designated point of contact for child safety matters is available to speak with authorities and regulators about our CSAM prevention practices.
