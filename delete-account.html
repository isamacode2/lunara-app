Account Management

Delete Your Account

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account at any time, or request deletion of specific data without closing your account.

Option 1 — Recommended

Delete via the App

The quickest way to delete your account is directly through the Lunara app. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

  1. Open the Lunara app and go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap Settings (top right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision — you'll receive a confirmation email
Option 2

Request via Email

If you can't access the app, email us and we'll process your deletion request within 30 days. Please include your registered email address.

📧 Email Deletion Request
Option 3 — Partial Data

Delete Specific Data Only

You can request deletion of specific data without closing your account. Email us specifying what you'd like removed — photos, voice intro, message history, etc.

📧 Request Data Removal
